Humorless Dictum

ATTENTION: URGENT MEMO

As a reminder to all crew, do NOT tell jokes to or around ANY mimes. On this ship, that means Tyne Dyer, Esq. or any of his PorOrCorps. The Humorless Dictum will require very, very strict punishment to any and all attempting to make a mime crack a smile, though fortunately for all of you capital punishment is only required for other mimes. The Dictum covers ALL things that resemble clowns, including but not limited to: humor, oversized shoes, non-medical nasal prosthetics, brightly colored suits, bizarre hair (see Regulation 23-7-8 for what qualifies as "bizarre"), and hand-held bicycle horns. We simply cannot risk any accidental feeding of Yottamince, and though they are opposed to her, the mimes are still spritual conduits to the Multiversal Beings. We may not be on the front lines, but we are still at war, and we must all do our part.

Lieutenant Redshirt of the Ogden

Classification level: Lemniscate
