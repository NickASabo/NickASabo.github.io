Turn 0

Welcome aboard, colleagues!

We here aboard The Ashbery FRP Department of Science and Exploration Accommodations and Docking Vessel welcome you to your new home, and congratulate both of your crews on the first successful missions to Skaoi IV. Both The Ogden and The Carroll will dock in just a few hours, and I want to make certain you know what to expect in the coming weeks.

You will be required to submit a scientific log of your findings over one aspect of the mission twice a week, including from crewmen. This may be concerning the ship or a part thereof, any aspect of the ruined civilization, details of your exploration, or just about anything. You may also submit a journal entry, part of a maintenance manual, or any evidence of interest. These should be short, from about 200 to 300 words. In the first 2 turns, a hard cap of 325 will be implemented to ease pressure on both you and your colleagues. After 8 articles, each member of the mission will be given an opprotunity to write a background, future plans, and/or obituary for themself.

You will be following strict instructions outlined here, using formatting found here. Please read these documents in detail on your own time. Note the details on citations and phantom citations. The details per turn will be reiterated when relevant. Finally, your letter index per turn will be assigned by me after successful receipt of a complete set.

Before you arrive, please have ready your identification card, credentials, and role aboard your respective ship, and submit those to me alone as soon as they are ready. They are due by Monday, and your first article by Wednesday.

Kelsey Khan, Director of Travels and Communications, The Ashbery, Federated Republic of Planets
