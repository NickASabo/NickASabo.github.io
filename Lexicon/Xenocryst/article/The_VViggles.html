You are members of the Carroll and Ogden research teams, freshly returned from simultaneous first expeditions to Skaoi IV, now aboard The Ashbery, a research travel hub for the Federated Republic of Planets.

The VViggles

The VViggles were one of the most popular scientific-rock bands ever in this galaxy. They have left a lasting legacy in the form of the Wiegler-Nrtan Vibrations that were named after the two founders of the band.

History
VVinny Wiegler and Tanner Nrtan met in an engineering class at 4th Planet Theoretical College and instantly connected over their passion for scientific-rock. They quickly formed a band, with VVinny on vocals and theremin and Tanner on quantum keyboard, and they recruited two other members for hypno-drums and acidic bass.

Influence
Following the propagation of WN Vibrations throughout the universe by their devoted fans (known as VVigglers), many institutions adopted VViggles tunes as theme songs. Examples include the CDS National Anthem, "All My Atoms Belong to You," the Imaginary Martian University Alma Mater, "Are You a Simulation?" and the commercial jingle for Unitied Astronomics.
